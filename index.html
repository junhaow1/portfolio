Why choose us?

Mojexa’s simple, unique location-based software lets you stay on top of your asset and property management workload by giving you access to the information you need anytime and anywhere. You can collaborate with your team, engage with your clients and communicate with your contractors to ensure things are running efficiently. 

Mojexa lets you offer contactless interactions with our Fix It™ system, helping to limit time spent onsite keeps us, our families, and our clients as safe as possible.

Mojexa Fix It™

The Mojexa Fix It™ system lets you use the camera on your device to quickly access details and documentation, record and respond to issues, and send notifications, giving you seamless engagement with other users in your Mojexa ecosystem.

Planned maintenance
